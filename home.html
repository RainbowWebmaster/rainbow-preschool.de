Welcome to Rainbow Preschool

Rainbow Preschool in Munich is a bilingual Montessori Preschool for children aged 3–6, supported by the City of Munich.

“The world is a rainbow with many kinds of people” – this line from a children’s song reflects the multicultural experience at Rainbow Preschool.

Educational Concept

Based on Maria Montessori’s methods, children are encouraged to be curious, take initiative, and discover independence and joy in learning.

What Makes Us Unique

  • Multicultural: Families of diverse ethnic and religious backgrounds
  • Daily Schedule: Montessori work, arts & crafts, circle time, outdoor play
  • Organic Food: Healthy snacks and lunch from Valerians Catering and Amperhof
  • Celebrations: Oktoberfest, St. Martin’s Day, Diwali, Chinese New Year, Halloween
  • Field Trips: Monthly visits to museums, farms, exhibitions
  • Library: Awarded “Gütesiegel Buchkindergarten” for promoting early reading

Opening Hours
