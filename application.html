Application

Admissions

If you wish to join our Rainbow family, please fill in our application form and send it to admissions@rainbow-preschool.de. We will reply as soon as possible.

Fees

  • €120 monthly membership fee
  • €65–75 monthly food fee (lunch and drinks)
  • €25 monthly fee for music class
  • €0 childcare fee*

*Rainbow Preschool e.V. is supported according to the EKI Plus model of the City of Munich. The childcare fees are based on the maximum permissible parental fees in accordance with the guideline for parental fee relief for parent-child initiatives (EKI) in the EKI support model. For further information please visit: https://www.muenchen.de/rathaus/Serviceangebote/familie/kinderbetreuung/kindertageseinrichtungen/traeger-stadt/kosten.html
